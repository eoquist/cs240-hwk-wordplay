How to Play:

  • You will be given a mystery 6-letter word. Your goal is to guess the word and all of its permutations. The mystery word will be scrambled and output to the console.

  • The total amount of words that you have to guess will be represented by dashes showing the total number of letters.

  • Your guesses should be 3 to 6 characters long.

  • If you input * as a guess, the mystery word's letter will be scrambled once again.

  • The game ends when all words are guessed or you press cancel instead of inputting a guess.
